Short group bio

Our group formed this autumn and it contains five members. We all met as part of our TKD studies and even though we all come from different backgrouds, so we bonded as the unexperienced and unpriviledged students with no prior experience in CSS and HTML. We worked together for the two mandatory assignments that we have been given, as well as for this one last step in completting DATA1200.
